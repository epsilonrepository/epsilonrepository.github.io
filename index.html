Welcome! I'm Edward, and this is my general-purpose repository.

I'll put anything substantial that I create here, whether I think it's good or bad or somewhere in-between. The repository is supposed to be all-encompassing but also organized. I also keep my blog here, which I view as the combination of a public diary, prose poetry, philosophical buds, and a dumping ground.

Unless I explicitly mark something otherwise, everything on here is my intellectual property. I store things here as a resource for curiosity and inspiration, not for theft.

Currently, I would prefer that you don't share this project. But once I die, feel free to share this far and wide!
