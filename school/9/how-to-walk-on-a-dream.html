How to Walk on a Dream

Creative Writing; based on the writing of Ben Purkert

let your neck be covered with eggshell clouds
surrounding your field of view in a calm white scramble
let your feet rub the rough black pavement
surrounding your soles in heavy falling pebbles
let your arms carry the weight of your mind
surrounding your fingers in the feather thoughts of the day
let your knees unbuckle and unstrain
surrounding your legs with the release of the night

POETRY / SCHOOL: GRADE 9
