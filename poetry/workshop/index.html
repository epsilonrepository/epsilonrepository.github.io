Poetry Workshop collection

December 2021 – March 2022

In the second trimester of my senior year, I took a poetry workshop elective where I wrote about a dozen poems. Some of them weren't absolutely terrible — I put those ones here. I've shared most of these poems with some people, but I certainly haven't shared all of them. I turned in the 5 poem culmination for my final project. Some of them were following a prompt, some were not.
