Welcome to my general-purpose repository!

Anything substantial I create I put here, whether I think it's good or bad or somewhere in-between. The repository is supposed to be all-encompassing but also organized. I also keep my blog here, which I view as the combination of a public diary, prose poetry, philosophical buds, and a dumping ground.

Unless I explicitly mark something otherwise, everything on here is my intellectual property. I store things here as a resource for curiosity and inspiration, not for theft.

Currently, I humbly request you don't share this project. Though once I die, I don't care — share it far and wide!
